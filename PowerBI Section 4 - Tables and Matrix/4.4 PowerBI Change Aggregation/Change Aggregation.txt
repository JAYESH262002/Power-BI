-> Open your report in Power BI and navigate to the visualization section.
-> Find the Fields pane and expand the Columns section.
-> Look for the name of the data used in a visual in the Columns section.
-> Beside the field name, you will see an up-side arrow symbol.
-> Click on the up-side arrow symbol to open the aggregation options for that field.
-> Select an aggregation option that is most suitable for your analysis.
-> The data in the visual will be updated according to the selected aggregation option.
